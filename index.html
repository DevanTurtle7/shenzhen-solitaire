How to Play

The goal of the game is to move all cards off the center, and into the upper left and right.

  • Move numbered cards to the upper-right
  • Move the 'Dragon' cards (with logos) to the upper-left.

Numbered Cards

Numbered cards can stack, with smaller numbers stacked on larger numbers.

Cards can't be stacked on Dragons, or on cards of the same suit.

Multiple number cards can be moved around if they're stacked correctly.

Dragon Cards

Dragon cards can't be stacked.

If the four dragons of a suit are uncovered and a slot is available, clicking the Dragon button moves that suit to a free slot in the upper-left.
